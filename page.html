Welcome to the CWG. In this game you are to pick your own story. Try your best to win!

You are in the old town of salem. It is a windy and spooky day. Rumors of the whitches blow in the wind. You are in the tavern and you meet a woman what do you do?

You introduce yourself. She relpies and says her name is Abigail. You two hit it off. She invites you to her home. What do you do?

You decide to keep to yourself and go home. You get a good nights rest. You wake up and hear something strange in your house. You go check it out. There is a woman your kitchen just standing there. What do you do?

You decline Abigails invite and head home. You wake wake up and Abigail is standing over you enraged. There is nothing you can do but lay there in shock.
